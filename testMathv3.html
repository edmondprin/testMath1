Math test - Kipp Paseo 1st Grade quiz

Exercice 1: Subtraction

John had trains at the beginning of the day, then realized he only got trains left after the garage sale. Do you know how many trains John sold on that day?

Exercice 2: Addition

Maia has dolls and Matteo has trucks. How many toys do Maia and Matteo have in total?

Exercice 3: Multiplication

Mrs Sarinana said she would give each student cents. There are students in the class. How many cents does Mrs Sarinana have to give to the whole class?
